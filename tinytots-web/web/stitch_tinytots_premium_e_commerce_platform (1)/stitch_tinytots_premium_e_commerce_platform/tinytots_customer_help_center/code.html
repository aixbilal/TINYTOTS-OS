Frequently Asked Questions

Delivery & COD

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days within major metropolitan areas. Rural deliveries may take an additional 2-3 days. You can track your exact timeline using your order number.

Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery for orders up to $500 in select zip codes. A nominal fee of $5 applies to COD orders. Please ensure you have exact change available.

Exchange Steps

To exchange an item, navigate to 'Initiate Returns' and select 'Exchange'. We will dispatch the new size as soon as your return package is scanned by our carrier. Exchanges are free within 30 days of purchase.

Fabric Guidelines

Yes, all our organic cotton garments are pre-washed to minimize shrinkage. However, we recommend washing on cold and tumble drying on low to maintain the shape and softness of the natural fibers.
