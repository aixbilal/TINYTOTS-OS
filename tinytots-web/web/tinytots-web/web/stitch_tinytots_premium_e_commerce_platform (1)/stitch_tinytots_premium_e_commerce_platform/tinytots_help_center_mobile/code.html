Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. Express options are available at checkout.

Is Cash on Delivery available?

Yes, COD is available for orders under $200 in select zip codes.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging.

Are your fabrics organic?

Most of our newborn collection is made from GOTS certified organic cotton. Please check individual product descriptions.
