1. Data Collection

At TinyTots, we prioritize the privacy and security of your family's information. We collect data necessary to provide our refined nursery services and process your orders efficiently. This includes personal identification information, browsing behavior, and purchase history.

The information we collect is utilized strictly for improving your experience, tailoring our recommendations, and ensuring seamless service delivery. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. For a comprehensive breakdown of our data handling practices, please review our detailed Information Security Guidelines.

2. Secure Payment

We offer secure, localized payment options to ensure a smooth checkout experience. All transactions are encrypted and processed through certified gateways.

By selecting a payment method, you authorize us to charge the specified amount for your selected services or products.

Creating an account with TinyTots allows you to manage bookings, track orders, and save your preferences. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate our terms of service or engage in fraudulent activities. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, please contact our support team immediately.

4. Delivery & COD Compliance

We strive to deliver your nursery essentials and consulting materials promptly and safely. Our delivery timelines vary based on location and product availability.

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, compliance is strictly enforced. You agree to be available at the designated delivery address to receive the order and complete the payment. Failure to comply with COD terms may result in restrictions on future orders.
